Android build remains blocked by missing local Android SDK. Feature research evidence is summarized in `docs/WHOORDAN_RESEARCHED_CALCULATIONS.md` and `docs/WHOORDAN_FEATURE_RESEARCH_AND_VALIDATION.md`.
